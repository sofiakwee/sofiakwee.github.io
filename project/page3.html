The Lanikai Beach is an amazing beach in Hawaii. Its stunning beach is over a mile long and has amazing swimming and snorkling. Some of the world's most beautiful fish can be seen here. This includes the Hawaiian state fish the Rhinecanthus rectangulus.


The beach Kaunaoa is a secluded paradice. It is a great place to relax on the beach with a good book. It is also a great location to meditate or do yoga. The calming sound of the waves create a relaxation destination.
