About Me

Hi! I am a rising junior at UC Berkeley pursuing a B.A in Data Science with a concentration in Computational Biology. Through my projects tracking ocean pollution, I discovered a passion for using Data Science in order to help my local and greater community. After conducting a machine learning project on the genetic composition of mice, I learned I could apply my technical experience to the field of medicine. My overall goal is to work in bioinformatics and use my skills to help my community have better access to quality healthcare.
